Compare Investment Home Loans

Investment Home Loans are designed exclusively for the purchase of real estate to be held for investment purposes only. Please note that it is not uncommon for the rate that applies to an investment loan to be slightly higher than for the same loan product if used for an owner occupied property.

There are two types of loans that are often used by investors of real estate:

1- Interest only loans:

Unlike a principal and interest home loan, interest only loans allow the investor to only pay the interest portion of the loan whilst leaving the loan principal untouched. A few benefits of interest only loans is the monthly repayments are less than they would be if you were to pay off principal as well, this means it is easier to turn the property into a cash flow positive property. Another major benefit is that you can get a tax deduction for all interest payments, unlike for the principal portion of your repayments.

2- Line of credit loans:

If you already own a real estate property, for example it could be your own home or another piece of real estate that is held for investment purposes, a line of credit offers you a way to access any equity you have built up in that property and, use it as a deposit for your next investment property.

A line of credit loan can also be used to make changes to your investment property allowing you to increase the rent you can charge on it. Naturally you can use this to for any additional expenses that may come up.
