Currently earning $670 Nett per week. Invest in a cash cow or move in.

For Sale $460k including Furniture.

Currently leased by Corporate Keys Australia who specialise in renting furnished apartments to their extensive database of corporate accounts, a savvy investor will be rewarded with rent of $670 per week with no management fees to be deducted from this amount. The property will be sold FULLY FURNISHED allowing you to continue with the agreement the owner has in place with Corporate Keys.

Completed BMT Tax Depreciation report will be provided to any purchasing investors showing available depreciation allowances of between $6769 and $6617 per year until 2019.

Looking to move in? The owner has secured a special release agreement with Corporate Keys should you wish to occupy the property. The choice is yours.

The apartment is in pristine condition having been maintained with regular housekeeping and carpet steam cleaning.

This inviting 35th floor apartment offers instant enjoyment of one of the inner city's most alluring addresses, between Crown Entertainment Complex and the vibrant Southgate precinct.

It presents state-of-the-art open plan interiors tailored to maximise space and natural light. Resort style facilities are available on the 10th floor which includes a fully equipped gym, huge 25-metre indoor pool, spa, sauna, theatre, amazing half acre gardens with numerous BBQ areas and a business centre & conference room

The tiled entrance hall leads into a large living and dining area with a glorious southwest aspect. Schemed in stone benchtops and mirror splashbacks, the sleek kitchen incorporates stainless steel SMEG appliances including an oven, cooktop, dishwasher and microwave. Behind the oversized sliding door is the double bedroom with generous built-in robes. There is also a beautiful fully tiled bathroom with a walk-in shower. Features include a European laundry, ducted reverse cycle air-conditioning. A integrated workstation/study nook is perfect for professionals.

Property is currently occupied so inspection is by appointment only. Property is being sold privately. Corporate Keys has simply listed this property on behalf of the property owner.
